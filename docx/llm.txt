Title: Large Language Models (LLMs)

Content:
Large Language Models (LLMs) are deep learning models trained on vast amounts of text data.

They use transformer architecture to understand and generate human-like text.

Popular LLMs include:
- GPT models
- FLAN-T5
- LLaMA
- Mistral

Key capabilities:
- Text generation
- Question answering
- Summarization
- Translation
- Code generation

Limitations:
- Can hallucinate incorrect facts
- Knowledge is limited to training data
- Sensitive to prompt design

LLMs are often combined with RAG to improve reliability.
