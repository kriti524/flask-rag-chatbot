Title: Transformers in NLP

Content:
Transformers are a type of neural network architecture introduced in the paper "Attention is All You Need".

They rely on a mechanism called self-attention to process input data.

Key components:
- Attention mechanism
- Encoder and decoder layers
- Positional encoding

Advantages:
- Parallel processing
- Better long-range dependency handling

Transformers are the foundation of modern NLP models like BERT and GPT.

They have significantly improved performance in tasks such as:
- Machine translation
- Text summarization
- Question answering
